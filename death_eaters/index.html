Voldemort

Voldemort (real name Tom Marvolo Riddle) is a character in the Harry Potter series of novels, the main antagonist. A descendant of Salazar Slytherin, one of the four founders of Hogwarts and his wife Caravella Aladeriva. One of the most famous dark wizards, possessing the greatest magical power and almost achieved immortality with the help of horcruxes.

Bellatrix Lestrange

A fictional character in the Harry Potter book series written by J.K. Rowling. She went from an unnamed minor character in Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire to the main antagonist in subsequent novels. In the final part of the story, Rowling called her Lord Voldemort's "last, best lieutenant." Bellatrix was the first female Death Eater featured in the books. Bellatrix was fanatically obsessed with the Dark Lord, although she was clearly afraid of his magical abilities and absolute power over his powers. She is almost as sadistic and a murderer as Lord Voldemort, with a psychopathic personality.

Lucius Malfoy
