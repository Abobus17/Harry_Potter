Severus Snape

Professor of potions (1981-1996) and defense against the Dark Arts (1996-1997) at the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Dean of the Slytherin Faculty in 1981-1997, director of Hogwarts in 1997-1998. A Death Eater and a member of the Order of the Phoenix at the same time, a double agent. He is also a brilliant potion maker and inventor of new spells. Despite a very difficult relationship with Harry Potter, he helped both him and other Gryffindors on the orders of the former director (before Snape) of Hogwarts, Albus Dumbledore. He died on May 2, 1998, in the Battle for Hogwarts, when Voldemort ordered his snake Nagaina to personally kill the professor.
